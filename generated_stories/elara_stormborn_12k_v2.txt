### Prologue - Ancient Prophecy Foretells Queen's Rise

In the heart of Atheria, a land where magic and steel coexist in eternal dance, there lies an ancient prophecy that speaks of a queen destined to rise from the storm. It is said she will unite five kingdoms against their greatest foe: Vexarion, the dragon who once ruled with fire and shadow before being sealed away by the ancients.

The prophecy reads:

"When shadows lengthen and ice grips the land,
A tempest shall arise, a queen of might and grand.
She'll wield lightning from skies, her heart as pure as snow,
To forge alliances where none thought they'd ever go."

Centuries have passed since Vexarion's defeat. The dragon remained imprisoned beneath the Thane Mountains until whispers began to stir in dark corners of Atheria about his return. This resurgence brought forth an urgency among those who had long safeguarded the ancient texts and lore.

Queen Elara Stormborn, ruler of Arvandor, was but a young maiden when she first learned of her lineage's connection to this prophecy. Her father, King Alaric, kept the knowledge hidden from all save his closest advisors—Theron Blackwood, an enigmatic man with ties to both shadow and light, and Dwarven King Borin Ironforge, whose realm lay deep beneath the mountains.

Elara’s mother was Lyralei Moonwhisper, a highborn elven princess who had fallen in love with Alaric against all odds. Her bloodline ensured that Elara possessed an innate connection to both magic and leadership, traits rare even among the powerful of Atheria.

As Vexarion's power grew once more, so too did the urgency for action. The queen’s advisors sensed a convergence of events that could either bring forth salvation or doom their world. Theron Blackwood had long studied ancient texts in search of answers; Borin Ironforge knew secrets buried deep within his dwarven kingdom about Vexarion's past and how to defeat him.

Elara, now fully aware of her destiny, began preparations for the inevitable conflict. She sought allies across borders traditionally wary of each other—humans, elves, dwarves—and even reached out to distant lands beyond known maps in search of warriors who could aid them against a foe older than memory itself.

Thus begins Elara's journey from queen to legend as she rallies not just armies but the very hearts and spirits of all Atheria. The storm is coming, and with it, so too will rise the light that shall vanquish darkness forevermore.
