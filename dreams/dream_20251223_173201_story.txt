   - Search queries
   - Social media interactions
   - Bookmarks and articles read

2. **Pattern Recognition**: Utilize machine learning algorithms to detect patterns in the user's behavior that indicate confirmation bias. This could include:
   - Seeking out information that confirms preexisting beliefs
   - Avoiding contradictory information
   - Engaging primarily with like-minded individuals or sources

   - Articles from reputable sources with opposing perspectives

6. **Feedback Loop**: Incorporate a feedback loop to refine the machine learning algorithms over time. Users can rate the effectiveness of suggested viewpoints, allowing the system to continuously improve its recommendations.
